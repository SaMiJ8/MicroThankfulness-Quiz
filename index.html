MicroThankfulness Quiz

Be thankful for every BIT!

This is a website for anyone who wants to make something to show someone that they're thankful! Using the Microbit, we made a mini interactive quiz that you can send to friends and family to show your thankful, and you can also personalize it.

Directions:
  • Click on the Quiz button below to make the quiz questions(max. 3) and answers!
  • Click on the Microbit logo below to make it fun, the instructions are in the comments of the Microbit code, If you would like to test it out first start with the simulator!
  • Send the link of your quiz to the ones you're thankful for today!
